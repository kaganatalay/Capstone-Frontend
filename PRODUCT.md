# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Young-adult and adult gift-buyers in Turkey (18–40), typically on mobile, mid-purchase of a special occasion (birthday, Valentine's, Mother's Day, graduation). They feel the anxiety of not knowing what to give: they want to be seen as thoughtful, not generic. They use this at the last moment or early in the decision process. Turkish-language audience; product UI is entirely in Turkish.

## Product Purpose

Gifty is an AI-powered gift recommendation tool. The user answers 14 short questions about the recipient and occasion; the AI returns a personalized ranked list of gift products with links to buy on Trendyol. Success means the user finds a gift they feel genuinely fits the person, not just a safe fallback. The emotional reward is confidence: "this gift will mean something."

## Brand Personality

Warm, precise, celebratory. Not generic. Not algorithmic-feeling. The brand should feel like a clever friend who knows exactly what to give — not a search engine dressed up as an advisor.

## Anti-references

- Amazon-style product search (cold, grid-of-results, utility-only)
- Generic AI chatbot UIs (flat, corporate, no personality)
- Pastel/millennial pink "gift guide" blogs (too soft, no precision)
- Heavy SaaS dashboards (this is a consumer moment, not a workspace)

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over choice paralysis** — fewer, better recommendations beat endless lists. Every element earns its place.
2. **Delight at the moment of reveal** — the results page is the climax; the entrance of recommendations should feel like unwrapping a gift.
3. **Premium without cold** — dark luxury palette conveys thoughtfulness and quality; warmth comes through gold accents and human copy, not pastel softness.
4. **Mobile-first, thumb-first** — the primary surface is a phone screen. No hover-dependent interactions, no copy that requires scrolling to reach the CTA.
5. **Trust through restraint** — no dark patterns, no fake urgency, no required accounts. The product earns trust by doing less and doing it well.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA as baseline; body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 against background
- Reduced motion respected (static alternatives for all animations)
- Turkish-language throughout; no Latin fallback strings in product UI
- Tap targets ≥ 44×44px on mobile
